# Investment Valuation

The definitive textbook on valuing assets. It covers various models (DCF, relative valuation) and how to apply them to different types of companies across all stages of the life cycle.

## Actionable Tips
1. Understand the difference between an asset's price and its intrinsic value.
2. Master the Discounted Cash Flow (DCF) model for fundamental analysis.
3. Adjust valuation models based on the company's life cycle stage.
4. Factor in macro-economic variables (interest rates, inflation) into your models.
5. Always question the assumptions built into any valuation metric.
