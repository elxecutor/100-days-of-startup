# Smartest Guys in the Room

A journalistic autopsy of the Enron scandal. It details how hubris, aggressive accounting loopholes, and a toxic corporate culture led to one of the biggest bankruptcies in history.

## Actionable Tips
1. If a company's financial statements are too complex to understand, avoid investing.
2. Beware of corporate cultures that reward aggressive risk-taking without oversight.
3. Look out for misaligned incentives between executives and shareholders.
4. Pay attention to cash flow rather than just reported accounting earnings.
5. Question the integrity of management if they constantly deflect criticism.
