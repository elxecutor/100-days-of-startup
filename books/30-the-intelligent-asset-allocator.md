# The Intelligent Asset Allocator

A quantitative look at portfolio construction. It mathematically demonstrates how diversifying across non-correlated asset classes reduces risk and improves returns over time.

## Actionable Tips
1. Focus entirely on your asset allocation strategy, not stock picking.
2. Diversify your portfolio across domestic, international, and bond markets.
3. Rebalance your portfolio strictly on a set schedule (e.g., annually).
4. Keep your investment costs and advisory fees as low as possible.
5. Understand that volatility is the price you pay for long-term returns.
