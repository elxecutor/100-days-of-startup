# The Hard Thing About Hard Things

A gritty, no-nonsense guide to being a CEO and surviving startup disasters. It offers practical advice on firing people, managing psychological meltdowns, and leading during wartime.

## Actionable Tips
1. Embrace the struggle; there is no formula for dealing with impossible situations.
2. Always tell the truth to your team, especially when things are going badly.
3. Train your employees thoroughly; it is the highest-leverage activity a manager can do.
4. When firing someone, do it quickly, respectfully, and take personal responsibility.
5. Separate your personal identity from your company's performance to protect your mental health.
