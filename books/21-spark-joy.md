# Spark Joy

An illustrated masterclass on the KonMari method of decluttering. It provides specific folding techniques, organizing strategies, and the philosophy of keeping only items that "spark joy." 

## Actionable Tips
1. Tidy by category (clothes, books, paper, etc.), not by location.
2. Hold every item in your hands to see if it sparks a physical response of joy.
3. Fold clothes vertically so you can see everything in your drawers.
4. Thank the items you discard for their service before letting them go.
5. Designate a specific, permanent home for every item you decide to keep.
