# Traction

Introduces the Entrepreneurial Operating System (EOS), a practical framework for business leaders to gain control of their companies, execute their vision, and build healthy leadership teams.

## Actionable Tips
1. Define a clear, shared vision and document it on a single page.
2. Ensure you have the right people in the right seats on your team.
3. Track 5-15 highly measurable weekly metrics (a scorecard) for the business.
4. Identify, discuss, and actually solve core issues rather than treating symptoms.
5. Implement structured, high-value weekly meetings (Level 10 meetings).
