# Trade Your Way to Financial Freedom

Focuses on the psychological and systemic aspects of trading. It emphasizes position sizing, risk management, and developing a trading system that fits your personality.

## Actionable Tips
1. Define your personal risk tolerance before making any trades.
2. Focus on position sizing rather than just entry signals.
3. Always use stop-losses to protect your capital.
4. Understand your system's "expectancy" (average win vs. average loss).
5. Keep a detailed trading journal to analyze your psychological triggers.
