# Getting Real

A guide to building web applications faster and with less complexity. It advocates for starting small, ignoring traditional project management bloat, and iterating quickly based on reality.

## Actionable Tips
1. Build less: strip your product down to its core utility.
2. Fund yourself to maintain control and avoid pleasing outside investors.
3. Fix the problem, not the blame, when things go wrong.
4. Launch quickly to get real-world feedback rather than endless planning.
5. Say no to feature requests unless they align perfectly with your core vision.
