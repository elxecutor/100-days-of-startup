# Growth Hacker Marketing

Replaces traditional marketing with a mindset focused on testable, scalable, and trackable growth. It emphasizes product-market fit and leveraging user behavior to drive virality.

## Actionable Tips
1. Ensure you have absolute Product-Market Fit before spending on marketing.
2. Build marketing and virality directly into the product experience.
3. Use data and A/B testing to guide your marketing decisions.
4. Focus on retaining existing customers as fiercely as acquiring new ones.
5. Find unconventional, low-cost channels to reach your target audience.
