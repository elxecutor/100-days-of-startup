# The Year of Less

A memoir documenting a 12-month shopping ban. The author discovers that consuming less leads to saving money, breaking bad habits, and finding fulfillment outside of consumerism.

## Actionable Tips
1. Do a complete inventory of what you already own before buying anything new.
2. Implement a 30-day "shopping ban" on non-essential items to reset habits.
3. Identify the emotional triggers that cause you to shop impulsively.
4. Unsubscribe from retail emails and delete shopping apps from your phone.
5. Repurpose or fix items instead of automatically replacing them.
