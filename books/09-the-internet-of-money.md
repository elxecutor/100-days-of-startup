# The Internet of Money

Explains the "why" of Bitcoin rather than just the "how." It frames cryptocurrency as a paradigm shift in trust, decentralized networks, and financial inclusion.

## Actionable Tips
1. View Bitcoin as a decentralized network, not just a digital stock.
2. Secure your own private keys (not your keys, not your coins).
3. Educate yourself on the underlying blockchain technology.
4. Consider the global implications of a borderless currency.
5. Start with a small, manageable investment to learn the mechanics.
