# Einstein: His Life and Universe

A comprehensive biography of Albert Einstein, exploring his scientific breakthroughs, rebellious nature, and how his imagination and non-conformity led to a revolution in physics.

## Actionable Tips
1. Nurture your curiosity; ask simple questions about how the world works.
2. Embrace thought experiments to solve complex problems visually.
3. Don't let rigid authority stifle your independent thinking.
4. Balance intense intellectual focus with hobbies (like playing music).
5. Accept that making mistakes is a natural part of groundbreaking work.
