# The Joy of Less

A minimalist guide to decluttering your home and your life. It provides a systematic approach to evaluating possessions, reducing stress, and making room for what truly matters.

## Actionable Tips
1. Use the "STREAMLINE" method to tackle one room at a time.
2. Ask yourself if an item is truly useful, beautiful, or loved.
3. Clear flat surfaces (counters, tables) to instantly reduce visual stress.
4. Implement a "one in, one out" rule for new purchases.
5. Limit sentimental items to a single, manageable memory box.
