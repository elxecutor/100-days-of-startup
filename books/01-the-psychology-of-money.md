# The Psychology of Money

Financial success is not a hard science; it’s a soft skill where how you behave is more important than what you know. This book explores how ego, preconceptions, and pride affect financial decisions.

## Actionable Tips
1. Focus on surviving financial downturns rather than just maximizing returns.
2. Define what "enough" means to you to avoid moving the goalposts.
3. Save money without needing a specific reason; build a buffer for the unpredictable.
4. Give yourself room for error in your financial projections.
5. Stop comparing your wealth to others; wealth is what you don't see.
