# The Daily Stoic

A daily devotional of Stoic meditations. It provides 366 short lessons on wisdom, perseverance, and the art of living, encouraging daily reflection and mindfulness.

## Actionable Tips
1. Start each morning by mentally preparing for the challenges of the day.
2. Journal your thoughts nightly to review your actions and reactions.
3. Pause before reacting to external provocations or insults.
4. Remind yourself daily of your mortality to appreciate the present moment.
5. Separate your self-worth from external validation or material success.
