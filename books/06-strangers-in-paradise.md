# Strangers in Paradise

Explores the psychological and social challenges faced by those who experience sudden wealth (inheritance, lottery, business sale) and how to navigate the transition into "Wealthland."

## Actionable Tips
1. Take a "decision-free zone" period for 6-12 months after acquiring sudden wealth.
2. Build a trusted team of financial and legal advisors.
3. Set clear boundaries with family and friends regarding money requests.
4. Educate yourself on the responsibilities of wealth management.
5. Find a new sense of purpose beyond just having money.
