# One Up On Wall Street

Argues that everyday investors can beat Wall Street professionals by using what they already know. Pay attention to local trends, new products, and companies you interact with daily.

## Actionable Tips
1. Invest in what you know and understand.
2. Look for "tenbaggers" (stocks that can multiply tenfold) in everyday life.
3. Categorize companies (fast growers, stalwarts, turnarounds) before investing.
4. Ignore short-term market fluctuations and focus on company fundamentals.
5. Don't invest in a company simply because the stock price is low.
