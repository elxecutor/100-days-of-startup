# The Power of a Positive No

Teaches how to say "no" effectively without destroying relationships. It introduces the "Yes-No-Yes" framework: affirming your core values, setting a boundary, and offering a constructive alternative.

## Actionable Tips
1. Identify your underlying "Yes" (your core values or priorities).
2. Deliver your "No" clearly, neutrally, and without excessive apologizing.
3. Follow up with a second "Yes" by proposing an alternative solution.
4. Separate the person from the problem during conflicts.
5. Pause and take a breath before responding to high-pressure demands.
