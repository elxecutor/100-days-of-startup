# Playing with FIRE (Financial Independence, Retire Early)

A personal narrative exploring the FIRE movement, detailing how ordinary people are aggressively saving and investing to retire decades earlier than the traditional age.

## Actionable Tips
1. Track every penny you spend to understand your true cost of living.
2. Optimize your "Big Three" expenses: housing, transportation, and food.
3. Increase your savings rate to 50% or more of your income.
4. Have open, honest conversations with your partner about financial goals.
5. Find joy in low-cost or free activities rather than consumerism.
