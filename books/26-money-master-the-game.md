# Money: Master the Game

Tony Robbins interviews top financial minds to distill their strategies into a 7-step playbook for financial freedom. It covers asset allocation, avoiding hidden fees, and creating lifelong income streams.

## Actionable Tips
1. Automate a specific percentage of your income into an investment account.
2. Educate yourself on the destructive power of high mutual fund fees.
3. Diversify your investments across different asset classes and risk buckets.
4. Consider establishing an annuity or income stream for retirement security.
5. Give back to others; true wealth includes a spirit of contribution.
