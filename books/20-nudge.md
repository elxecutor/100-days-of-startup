# Nudge

Explores choice architecture and behavioral economics. It explains how subtle changes in the way options are presented can drastically influence people's decisions regarding health, wealth, and happiness.

## Actionable Tips
1. Set up automatic enrollments for your savings and retirement accounts.
2. Design your environment to make good choices easier and bad ones harder.
3. Use pre-commitments to lock yourself into future positive behaviors.
4. Recognize your cognitive biases, like loss aversion and status quo bias.
5. Simplify complex information when trying to make optimal decisions.
