# The Global Expatriate's Guide to Investing

A practical guide for expats who cannot rely on their home country's retirement systems. It outlines how to build a robust, tax-efficient portfolio while living abroad.

## Actionable Tips
1. Open an offshore brokerage account that accepts non-residents.
2. Build a globally diversified portfolio using low-cost ETFs.
3. Avoid high-fee, offshore investment bonds often sold to expats.
4. Understand the tax implications of your home country and residence country.
5. Rebalance your portfolio annually to maintain your target asset allocation.
