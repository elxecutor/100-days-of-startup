# The Geometry of Wealth

Focuses on the intersection of money and meaning. It argues that true wealth is not just an accumulation of assets, but funding a life of contentment and purpose.

## Actionable Tips
1. Clearly define your personal values before setting financial goals.
2. Shift your focus from getting rich to funding a meaningful life.
3. Manage your behavior during market panics and manias.
4. Align your daily spending with your long-term priorities.
5. Recognize that money is a tool for time, not an end in itself.
