# The Basics of Bitcoins and Blockchains

An accessible introduction to the world of cryptocurrencies. It explains cryptographic hashes, how blockchains function, the history of digital money, and how to safely buy and store crypto.

## Actionable Tips
1. Understand the difference between Bitcoin (currency) and Blockchain (technology).
2. Never invest money in crypto that you cannot afford to lose entirely.
3. Use two-factor authentication (2FA) on all your crypto exchange accounts.
4. Move long-term holdings off exchanges and into a hardware wallet.
5. Do your own research (DYOR) before investing in alternative coins (altcoins).
