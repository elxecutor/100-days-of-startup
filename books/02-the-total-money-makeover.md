# The Total Money Makeover

A straightforward, step-by-step plan to get out of debt and build wealth using the "Baby Steps" method, emphasizing intense focus ("gazelle intensity") and behavioral change over mathematical formulas.

## Actionable Tips
1. Save a $1,000 starter emergency fund immediately.
2. Use the debt snowball method: pay off debts from smallest to largest balance.
3. Build a fully funded emergency fund of 3-6 months of expenses.
4. Invest 15% of your household income into retirement accounts.
5. Pay off your home mortgage early to achieve total financial freedom.
