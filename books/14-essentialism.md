# Essentialism

The disciplined pursuit of less. It teaches how to filter out the noise, say no to non-essential tasks, and focus exclusively on what truly matters to maximize your contribution.

## Actionable Tips
1. Conduct a regular audit of your time and commitments.
2. Practice saying "no" gracefully but firmly to non-essential requests.
3. Create a buffer in your schedule for thinking and resting.
4. Edit your life: if it isn't a clear "yes," then it's a "no."
5. Focus on doing one thing excellently rather than ten things poorly.
