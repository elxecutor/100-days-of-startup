# Principles of Economics

A foundational text outlining how markets work, how individuals make decisions based on incentives, and how macroeconomic forces like inflation and trade affect global prosperity.

## Actionable Tips
1. Always consider the opportunity cost of any decision you make.
2. Recognize that people respond predictably to incentives.
3. Understand that trade can make everyone better off through specialization.
4. Look beyond immediate effects to see the long-term consequences of policies.
5. Apply the concept of marginal thinking to everyday choices.
