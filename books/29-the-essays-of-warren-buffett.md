# The Essays of Warren Buffett

A curated collection of Buffett’s letters to Berkshire Hathaway shareholders. It serves as an advanced masterclass in corporate governance, capital allocation, and long-term value investing.

## Actionable Tips
1. Invest in businesses with a durable competitive advantage (a "moat").
2. Demand a margin of safety in every investment you make.
3. Evaluate management based on their capital allocation skills and integrity.
4. Ignore market volatility; Mr. Market is your servant, not your master.
5. View buying stock as owning a piece of a business, not a speculative ticker.
