# Venture Deals

A tactical guide to venture capital term sheets. It demystifies the negotiation process between founders and investors, explaining the economic and control dynamics of startup funding.

## Actionable Tips
1. Focus your negotiations on two main things: economics and control.
2. Understand how liquidation preferences affect your potential payout.
3. Hire a lawyer who specifically specializes in venture capital deals.
4. Build a competitive dynamic between investors to get the best terms.
5. Don't get overly hung up on valuation at the expense of bad terms.
