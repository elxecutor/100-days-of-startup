# The Simple Path to Wealth

Investing doesn't have to be complex. This book advocates for a minimalist approach to investing, primarily using low-cost broad market index funds (like VTSAX) to achieve financial independence.

## Actionable Tips
1. Avoid debt like the plague; it destroys wealth-building potential.
2. Spend less than you earn and invest the surplus heavily.
3. Invest in low-cost, broad-market index funds and hold them forever.
4. Ignore market volatility and financial news; stay the course.
5. Build "F-You Money" to buy back your freedom and time.
