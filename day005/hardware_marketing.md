# Hardware Marketing — Day 005

Objective: position the ECG frontend board as a low-power, developer-friendly module for early adopters and makers.

Target audience:
- Wearable hardware makers and researchers
- Early-stage digital health startups
- Hardware accelerators and university labs

Channels & tactics:
- Product Hunt / Maker News: short launch post with board photos and a small demo video.
- Twitter/X & LinkedIn: technical snapshot + measurement capture (oscilloscope screenshot) and BOM highlights.
- Hackster/Thingiverse: build guide and downloadable Gerbers.

Key messages:
- Low-power operation for multi-day prototyping.
- Clean ECG bandpass 0.5–40 Hz out of the box.
- Simple interface: single-supply, exposed test points, and example firmware.

Assets needed:
- High-res product photos, 20–30s demo video, quickstart guide, and a one-page spec sheet.

Launch timeline (2 weeks):
1. Prep assets and write documentation — 3 days
2. Soft launch to beta testers & maker communities — 4 days
3. Public launch with open files and tutorial — 7 days
