SaaS / LLM Prospecting — Templates

Cold email (short):
Subject: Quick idea to reduce [metric] at [Company]

Hi [Name],

I build [SaaS/LLM product] that helps [role] reduce [pain] by [result]. We recently helped [client] cut [metric] by [X%]. Can I share a 2-minute demo this week?

Thanks,
[Your name] — [Company]

LinkedIn connection + follow-up:
Connection request:
Hi [Name], I build tools that help [role] at [company] improve [metric]. Would love to connect.

Follow-up after connect:
Thanks for connecting — would you be open to a 10-min chat about reducing [pain] at [Company]? I can tailor a quick example to your stack.

Instagram DM (short):
Hey [Name], I noticed your work on [area]. I built an LLM tool that automates [specific task] — can I DM a 60s demo?

Personalization tokens to use: [Name], [Company], [role], [metric/pain], [one-line proof]

A/B testing tip:
- Subject A: "Quick idea to reduce [metric] at [Company]"
- Subject B: "[Company] + [Your product]: 10-minute demo"
- CTA test: "Schedule demo" vs "Send a 2-min screen recording"

Cold outreach best practices (brief):
- Keep first message ≤ 3 sentences.
- Include a measurable outcome or proof.
- Ask for a tiny next step (2–10 minutes).
